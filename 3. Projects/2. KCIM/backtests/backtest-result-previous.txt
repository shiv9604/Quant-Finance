## WHAT CHANGED IN THE NEW CODE (what to test for improvement)
The new KCIM.py has two major upgrades vs this baseline:

1. NEW detect_consolidation (visual 5m range-based):
   - Old: 3m candles, must close within ±5pts of 25th/75th percentile level
   - New: 5m candles, sliding window — zone range ≤40pts, avg ATR ≤20, max body ≤15pts
   - Expected effect: fewer signals but higher quality, more visual accuracy

2. NEW options premium filter (high/low instead of close only):
   - Old: range = (close.max - close.min) / close.mean
   - New: range = (high.max - low.min) / close.mean  ← captures wicks
   - Expected effect: stricter filter, blocks more trades where options were already moving
